tcs new york marathon new balance

The TCS New York City Marathon Expo Presented by New Balance is one of the country's largest running expositions, with an array of New Balance gear and accessories including the official TCS New York City Marathon collection, and course amenities offered by our partners. There are also numerous vendors and exhibitors, the Runners' Wall featuring all 50,000+ registered entrants, the NYRR Running Lab featuring tips and insights from our coaches, and more.


Visit the Mother information booth at the TCS New York City Marathon Expo Presented by New Balance to find out how NYRR is working with &Mother, a nonprofit driving change and building equality for mothers in sports, to further support nursing mothers participating in the TCS New York City Marathon by elevating its lactation experience with the aim to remove barriers to full participation. The information booth will help ensure runners are aware of the accommodations and preparation for medical device operations, and will include a lactation space for nursing parents to use within the booth.
